Chess

Chess is one of my favourite sports. I am an active participant in chess.com [account] and lichess.org [account]. I like to play it casually and I enjoy playing games with fast time control (as they are more fun).

I am an Associate Member Chess Club IIT Kharagpur since January 2021. I have been a part of the Tactics Team and the World Championship 2021 Coverage Editing Team of this society.



Indian Classical Music (Vocal)

I have been seeking vocal training in Indian Classical Music since 2005. Since 2009, I have been learning under the guidance and blessings of my guru, Vidushi Sangeeta Bandhyopadhyay. The training primarily consisted of training in various ragas. Some of my performances over the years can be found below:

  • Raag Puriya Dhaneshree (2014)
  • Raag Bhoopali Part 1, Part 2 (2015)
  • Raag Kedar (2015)
  • Raag Behag (2017)
  • Raag Rageshree (2022)

I was also a part of National Cultural Appreciation of IIT Kharagpur (March 2021 - Dec 2021) and a part of the group Sargam IITKGP.
